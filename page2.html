IoT Devices

IoT devices are physical devices connected to the internet, equipped with sensors and software to collect, exchange, and act on data.

These devices can range from consumer electronics like smart home appliances and wearables to industrial machines and infrastructure components.

Example of IoT Devices:

  • Smart Thermostats
  • Connected Vehicles
  • Industrial Sensors

IoT Applications:

IoT is used in various applications such as:

  • Smart Cities
  • Healthcare Monitoring
  • Industrial Automation
